Desmond Aldridge

...the coding student

Desmond is excited to embrace this new chapter in life, learning to become a fullstack developer.

Having self-taught(learned) html at the age of 13 through chatrooms (and eventually myspace), Desmond went on to take an html and two C++ courses in high-school.

However, Desmond pursued his passion in theater and story-telling, and went on to be an actor and live show performer.

Often thinking back to his teenage interest in coding (and linguistics in his 20s) the perfect storm of being an out of work entertainer, in quarantine, high regards towards producing digital content, a general nostalgic sentiment towards coding, and his mother and sister's start-up suddenly needing a new software developer... Desmond lept at the opportunity.

Thrilled to see where this new career path will lead, and how it could potentially enhance and incorporate his creative and artistic leanings.
